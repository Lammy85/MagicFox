Try to defeat as many moster as possible. You can only hit them while you are rolling. But beware, rolling costs energy. You can restore energy with resting. Each defeated monster earns you one score point. If a moster touches you without you rolling, you lose one life and one score point.
The game ends when the time is up or you have no more lives left.

Controls:

Arrow left/right: moving
Arrow up: jumping
Enter: roll
Arrow down(while on ground): resting
Arrow down(while in the air): special attack
p: pause
r: restart

This game is based on a tutorial from the YouTube channel Franks laboratory. I drew the spritesheets myself (except for the glitter that appears when you roll), but they are based on the spritesheets provided in the tutorial, which are created by the awesome artist bevouliin.
